# Cassidy Harden ♡

A phone-first gift site, built by her husband. Light, blush, girly, and full
of easter eggs. Plain HTML/CSS/JS — no build step.

## What's inside

- **Hero** — falling petals, polaroid photo, tap anywhere for sparkle bursts
- **Live counter** — days married since 4.5.25; tap the card to cycle units
  (days → hours → minutes → seconds)
- **Her story timeline** — Troy → A&M '25 → the Hawaii proposal → the wedding →
  TWU OTD '28, with a real-time "Dr. Harden loading" progress bar
- **Polaroid gallery** — swipeable, 12 photos from the wedding site
- **Scratch card** — scratch off with a finger to reveal a note
- **Open when… envelopes** — tap to flip open three notes

## Easter eggs

1. Tap her name 5× fast → confetti storm + secret message
2. Tap the "☀️ be the light" chip 3× → the whole page glows
3. Tap the counter to the "seconds" unit → bonus toast
4. Finish the scratch card → sparkle burst
5. Scroll to the absolute bottom → hidden last line

## Editing

- Photos live in `photos/` (pulled from the public Zola gallery).
- The scratch-card note and envelope notes are plain text in `index.html`.
- Wedding date/school dates are constants at the top of `script.js`.
